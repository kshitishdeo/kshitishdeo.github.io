  2. Sepsis Watch

    1. The Sepsis Watch application predicts the values of six labs which are advance indicators of sepsis. Gaussian process prediction model was developed for white blood counts, hemoglobin, bilirubin, platelets, creatinine, and lactate.
    2. The application, after a physician and patient are selected, launches the display of six screens with both the actual and predicted lab values.
    3. When a lab prediction is in the critical range, a mobile message is sent to the physician.
